Chinh sach Bao mat

Cap nhat lan cuoi: 10/05/2026

1. Thu thap thong tin

TechStore thu thap thong tin tu nguoi dung khi:

  • Dang ky tai khoan: ten dang nhap, email, mat khau, ho ten, so dien thoai, dia chi.
  • Dat hang: thong tin giao hang, lich su mua hang, phuong thuc thanh toan.
  • Su dung chatbot: cac cau hoi, yeu cau ho tro.
  • Duy tri trinh duyet: cookie de luu phien lam viec.

2. Su dung thong tin

Thong tin duoc su dung de:

  • Xu ly don hang va giao hang san pham.
  • Ho tro khach hang va tu van san pham.
  • Gui thong bao ve don hang, khuyen mai (co su dong y).
  • Cai thien chat luong dich vu va trai nghiem nguoi dung.

3. Bao mat thong tin

Chung toi cam ket bao mat thong tin nguoi dung bang cach:

  • Mat khau duoc ma hoa bang thuat toan bcrypt.
  • Du lieu duoc luu tren may chu an toan voi backup deu deu.
  • Chi nhan vien uy quyen moi co the truy cap du lieu khach hang.
  • Khong chia se thong tin khach hang cho ben thu ba khi khong co su dong y.

4. Quyen cua nguoi dung

Nguoi dung co quyen:

  • Truy cap va xem thong tin ca nhan da dang ky.
  • Yeu cau chinh sua thong tin ca nhan.
  • Yeu cau xoa tai khoan va du lieu lien quan.
  • Tu choi nhan email marketing bat cu luc nao.

5. Cookie

Website su dung cookie de:

  • Luu phien dang nhap va gio hang (khong can dang nhap).
  • Ghi nho phien lam viec nguoi dung.
  • Phan tich luu luong truy cap (Google Analytics).
