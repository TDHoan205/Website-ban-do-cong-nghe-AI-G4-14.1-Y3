=============================
CAU TRUC THU MUC DU AN
Tech Store AI (FastAPI + MVC)
=============================

1) Tong quan

app.py
- Diem vao FastAPI. Khoi tao app, dang ky routes, middleware, template engine, static files.
- Noi tap trung cac include_router/route tu Controllers/*.

requirements.txt
- Danh sach thu vien Python can thiet (FastAPI, SQLAlchemy, Jinja2, pyodbc, ...).

README.md
- Mo ta du an, huong dan cai dat, chay ung dung, cau hinh DB.

LICENSE
- Giay phep su dung ma nguon.


2) Thu muc chinh

Controllers/
- Tang Controller (Xu ly HTTP request/response).
- Moi Controller quan ly mot nhom route theo chuc nang.
- Nen code:
	* Nhan request, validate tham so, goi Service tuong ung.
	* Xu ly loi/ngoaile, tra ve TemplateResponse/JSON.
	* Khong chua logic nghiep vu phuc tap.

	Vi du:
	- HomeController.py: trang chu, landing, thong tin chung.
	- AuthController.py: dang nhap/dang ky, logout.
	- ProductsController.py: danh sach/chi tiet san pham.
	- CartController.py, CartItemsController.py: gio hang.
	- OrdersController.py, OrderItemsController.py: don hang.
	- ChatController.py: AI chat.
	- StatisticsController.py, InventoryController.py, SuppliersController.py, ...: quan tri.

Services/
- Tang Service (Nghiep vu).
- Noi xu ly logic chinh: tinh toan, goi DB, goi AI, thong ke.
- Nen code:
	* Ham xu ly nghiep vu co the test duoc.
	* Goi Models/ + Data/database.py de truy van.
	* Goi AI/ neu can, tra ve ket qua cho Controller.

	Services/AI/
	- Chua tien trinh lien quan AI (Gemini, RAG, embedding, ...).
	- Nen code: client goi API, pipeline tim kiem, xu ly prompt.

Models/
- Tang Model/Entity (SQLAlchemy ORM).
- Dinh nghia bang DB: cot, kieu du lieu, quan he.
- Nen code:
	* Lop ORM cho Product, Category, Order, User, Chat, ...
	* Dinh nghia relationship va rang buoc.

Data/
- Cau hinh ket noi DB va phan seed du lieu.
- Nen code:
	* database.py: engine, SessionLocal, ket noi SQL Server.
	* seed_data.py (neu co): du lieu mau ban dau.

SQL/
- File SQL khoi tao/seed DB.
- schema.sql: tao bang, khoa, rang buoc.
- seed_data.sql: du lieu mau (neu su dung truc tiep SQL).

Utilities/
- Tien ich dung chung, khong phu thuoc nghiep vu cu the.
- Nen code:
	* auth.py: JWT, hash password, decode token.
	* PagedList.py: phan trang, helper chung.

Views/
- Template Jinja2 cho giao dien.
- Shared/base.html: layout chung.
- Auth/, Home/, Products/, Cart/, Chat/: trang chuc nang.
- Nen code:
	* HTML/Jinja, vong lap, dieu kien, nhan du lieu tu Controller.

wwwroot/
- Tai nguyen tinh.
- css/style.css: giao dien.
- js/main.js: JavaScript client-side.
- images/: hinh anh.


3) Goi y luong xu ly

Request -> Controller -> Service -> Model/Data -> Controller -> View/JSON -> Response


4) Ghi chu

- Neu them tinh nang moi: tao route o Controller, xu ly o Service, cap nhat Model (neu DB thay doi),
	them View (neu can), va them CSS/JS o wwwroot.
- Han che nhung logic phuc tap trong Controller; day ve Service.
